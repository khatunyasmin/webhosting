OOTY

Ooty is a city in India.
It comes under the Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu. Ooty is a popular hill station. It is also known as the ‘Queen of Hills Station’ and the ‘Western Ghats’. Ooty is officially known as Udhagam Andalam and Ootacamund. The official language of Ooty is Tamil. The economy of Ooty is based on tourism and agriculture. Some popular places in Ooty are – the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, Pykara Lake, Pyakara Falls, Doddabetta peak, Avalanche Lake, Government Rose Garden, and Tea Museum. It is known to produce the best aromatic essential oils like eucalyptus, citronella, and camphor. It is located at an altitude of 2240 meters (7,350 ft) above sea level.
